Spaghetti Carbonara (Italian)
1. Boil water in a pot and cook 400g spaghetti until al dente.
2. Fry 150g pancetta until crispy in a pan.
3. Beat 2 eggs and mix with 50g grated Parmesan cheese.
4. Drain spaghetti and immediately mix with the pancetta.
5. Remove from heat and add the egg-cheese mixture, stirring quickly.
6. Season with black pepper and serve hot.

Chicken Tikka Masala (Indian)
1. Marinate 500g chicken in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices for 4 hours.
2. Grill or pan-fry the marinated chicken until cooked.
3. In a pan, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil.
4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft, then blend into a smooth sauce.
5. Add cream, spices, and grilled chicken to the sauce.
6. Simmer for 10 minutes and serve with rice or naan.

Beef Tacos (Mexican)
1. Cook 500g ground beef in a skillet until browned.
2. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water, simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Warm taco shells in an oven or pan.
4. Fill shells with beef, shredded lettuce, cheese, and salsa.
5. Garnish with sour cream and chopped cilantro.
6. Serve immediately.

Sushi Rolls (Japanese)
1. Rinse and cook sushi rice, then season with rice vinegar.
2. Lay nori on a bamboo mat and spread rice evenly on it.
3. Add slices of fish or vegetables in a line.
4. Roll the mat tightly to form the sushi roll.
5. Slice into pieces using a sharp knife.
6. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Pad Thai (Thai)
1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft.
2. Stir-fry tofu or shrimp in oil with garlic.
3. Add noodles and tamarind paste, fish sauce, sugar, and chili.
4. Push aside and scramble an egg in the pan.
5. Mix everything and add bean sprouts and peanuts.
6. Serve with lime wedges and green onions.

French Onion Soup (French)
1. Slice and caramelize onions in butter over low heat.
2. Add beef broth, thyme, and a splash of wine.
3. Simmer for 30 minutes.
4. Pour into bowls and top with toasted baguette slices.
5. Sprinkle grated Gruyère cheese on top.
6. Broil until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Kimchi Fried Rice (Korean)
1. Sauté chopped kimchi and garlic in oil.
2. Add cooked rice and stir-fry.
3. Mix in soy sauce, sesame oil, and gochujang.
4. Push rice aside and fry an egg in the same pan.
5. Mix egg into rice or serve it on top.
6. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.

Shakshuka (Middle Eastern)
1. Sauté onions, garlic, and bell peppers in olive oil.
2. Add canned tomatoes and spices (cumin, paprika).
3. Simmer until thickened.
4. Make wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them.
5. Cover and cook until eggs are set.
6. Serve with bread.

Butter Croissants (French)
1. Mix flour, sugar, yeast, and salt; add milk to make dough.
2. Roll dough and wrap butter inside, folding and rolling repeatedly.
3. Chill dough between folds to keep butter solid.
4. Roll out and cut into triangles, roll into crescent shapes.
5. Let rise for 2 hours.
6. Bake at 375°F (190°C) until golden.

Falafel Wraps (Middle Eastern)
1. Soak and blend chickpeas with herbs, garlic, onion, and spices.
2. Form into balls and fry until golden.
3. Warm pita bread or flatbread.
4. Spread hummus, add falafel, tomatoes, cucumber, and lettuce.
5. Drizzle with tahini sauce.
6. Roll into wraps and serve.
